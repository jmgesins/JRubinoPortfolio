Professional Summary & the Road to Grand Circus

After graduating with a Bachelor's of English from Oakland University in 2008, I began working at a digital marketing agency in Rochester, Michigan. Initially, my tasks included writing and editing copy for websites, eventually evolving into on and off-page SEO.

In 2010, I re-located to Chicago with my husband for a few months while continuing to freelance for companies as a digital marketer. Upon my return to Michigan, I continued working as a digital marketer for a few different companies, including a B2B company and a flooring retail store.

Today, I work as a digital marketer at an indirect auto finance company located in Southfield, Michigan. As my responsibilities within the digital marketing field evolve, I find myself needing more and more programming knowledge. In 2016, with my manager's blessing, I enrolled in the Grand Circus Bootcamp.

Let the fun begin!
